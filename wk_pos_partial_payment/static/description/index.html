POS Partial Payment

This module allows the seller to invoice and validate partially paid or unpaid orders in POS.

Salient Features

  • Seller can invoice and validate partially paid orders in POS.
  • Seller can later register payments in the corresponding invoice from backend.
  • Seller can set payment terms to deny partial payment for particular customers.
  • Seller needs to enter a remark/description while validating partially paid orders.
